How CrisisConnect Works

Powered by Gemini AI for intelligent emergency coordination

Report Incidents

Quickly report emergencies with location data and crucial details. Our AI system automatically categorizes and prioritizes incidents.

AI Coordination

Gemini AI analyzes incidents, matches resources to needs, and sends targeted alerts. Get real-time decision support based on emerging conditions.

Community Response

Volunteers, emergency personnel, and community members work together through a unified platform for coordinated disaster response.

CrisisConnect provides real-time visualization of incidents on an interactive map. See emerging situations, track response efforts, and coordinate effectively with responders in your area.
